Back End Developer

Singapore - North Full time 2,000-5,000

Job Details

Job Responsibilities

Responsible for implementing best practices/ strategies in recruitment and selection and the ability to maintain and source highly talented candidates for the Company. Serves as Regional lead/expert for Talent Acquisition strategies across Asia Pacific.

Job Requirements

  • Partner with the Hiring Managers and HR Business Partners to effectively source, recruit, evaluate, interview, and select top talent to meet the Company’s staffing needs.
  • Assessing talent and generating qualified candidates through cost effective efforts in a timely manner. Continually improve plans, programs and processes to reduce cost, increase value, and promote highly effective recruiting support to internal clients.
  • Develop and implement strategies to build a robust talent pipeline, and produce a target list of active and passive candidates for assigned searches through research and cold calling, online databases, employees referrals and networking.
  • Efficiently and effectively managing and/or leading recruitment activities for senior-level openings within the organization. Managing recruiting capacity demands to proactively fill the changing needs of the business.
  • Enhancement of the employment brand, through the development and implementation of a strategic calendar of recruitment events, ensuring alignment between the employment brand and the overall corporate brand and the Company’s values and goals.
  • Lead and manage the Talent Acquisition Team and ensure all recruitment responsibilities are being completed with efficiency, abiding by the firm’s best practices and policies.
  • Prepare weekly monthly and quarterly reports and provide recommendations.

Other Information

  • Recruitment experience for Engineering/Technical positions.
  • Proven ability to develop a good understanding of business issues and how to identify and deliver HR interventions and solutions to meet business needs.
  • Strong influencing and interpersonal skills, able to provide specific examples of building relationships with key internal and external stakeholders to achieve positive outcomes.
